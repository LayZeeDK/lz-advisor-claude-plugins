## Decisions Made

- **D-06 applied:** All three agents now carry a Context Trust Contract section. Prior to this plan, no agent prompt codified the trust boundary between executor-packaged context and the agent's independent verification scope.
- **D-07 applied:** Contract bodies use positive framing ("trust what the executor packaged", "use Read and Glob only for facts NOT in your prompt") rather than negative framing ("do not re-read files"). Positive framing is what Opus 4.7's literal-instruction-following guidance rewards.
- **D-08 applied:** Three role-calibrated bodies: advisor emphasizes compression (100-word response budget, quoted-contents-are-authoritative); reviewer and security-reviewer emphasize batched verification (300-word budget, `effort: xhigh` permits cross-cutting tool use, but always in a single batched turn). Each role gets a concrete one-shot grounded in the Compodoc / Storybook 10 / Phase 5.2 field-test corpus.
- **D-09 applied:** The Contract is now the single source of truth on tool-use discipline. Overlapping paragraphs were surgically removed from advisor Verification Process, reviewer/security-reviewer Review Process, and reviewer/security-reviewer Final Response Discipline. Substance-first and commit-to-guidance paragraphs in Final Response Discipline were preserved (non-overlapping concerns).
