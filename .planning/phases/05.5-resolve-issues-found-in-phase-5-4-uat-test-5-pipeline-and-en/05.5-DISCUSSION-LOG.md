### Clarification exchange (before pass-gate question)

**Context:** The first pass-gate question listed "execute <=1 advisor call" as the recommended gate. User rejected the AskUserQuestion and asked: "Why should the executor only call the advisor 1 time (at most)?"

**Correction:** The execute skill has three designed consultation points (Phase 2 pre-execute always, Phase 3 mid-execute conditional, Phase 5 final-review always). Minimum legitimate happy-path execute session = 2 calls, not 1. S5's 3 calls = 1 extra mid-execute call driven by avoidable Nx-convention gap. Correct gate is <=2 for execute, not <=1.
