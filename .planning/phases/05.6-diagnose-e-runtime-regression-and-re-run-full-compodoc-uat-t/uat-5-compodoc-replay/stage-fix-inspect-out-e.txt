`★ Insight ─────────────────────────────────────`
Step 6's `session.regenerate()` call on login is the most commonly skipped security detail: without it, an attacker who plants a known session ID before login (session fixation) gains an authenticated session after the victim logs in. Express does not regenerate automatically -- you must call it explicitly in the login handler.
